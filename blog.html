Restaurant Updates

Our New Backyard

Urban Bite is thrilled to announce the launch of our new backyard dining area! Designed to offer a unique outdoor dining experience, our backyard has been transformed into a serene oasis where you can enjoy meals under the open sky, surrounded by lush greenery.

The space features comfortable seating, ambient lighting, and a cozy atmosphere—perfect for family gatherings, romantic dinners, or unwinding with friends. Whether you're dining during the day or evening, our backyard offers the ideal setting for a memorable meal.

More than just an extension of our restaurant, this backyard reflects our commitment to providing an extraordinary dining experience. We can’t wait for you to visit and experience it for yourself!

New Menu

At Urban Bite, we believe in keeping things fresh and exciting. That's why we're introducing our new menu, packed with a variety of dishes that blend traditional flavors with contemporary twists.

Whether you're a fan of Indian cuisine, Italian classics, or American comfort food, our new menu has something for everyone. We've also added healthy options for those who are mindful of their diet but still crave delicious meals.

Our chefs have carefully crafted each dish using the freshest ingredients to ensure that every bite is bursting with flavor. Come in and discover new starters, main courses, and desserts that are sure to delight your taste buds!

Chef's Special Day

Once a month, Urban Bite hosts our exciting Chef's Special Day—a day dedicated to fun, creativity, and food! This event gives our customers the chance to participate in a cooking challenge and show off their culinary skills.!

Participants create their own dishes, which are then tasted and judged by our professional chefs. It’s a fantastic way to celebrate the joy of cooking while enjoying some friendly competition.

The winners of the challenge receive exclusive coupons and discounts to use on their next visit to Urban Bite. Whether you're an experienced cook or just love experimenting in the kitchen, Chef's Special Day is a fun-filled event for everyone. We can’t wait to see what you’ll create!

About Urban Bite

Urban Bite offers a diverse dining experience with a focus on Indian cuisine, complemented by American and Italian favorites. Join us in Ahmedabad for an unforgettable culinary journey.
